our values

grace

A quality of moving in a smooth, relaxed, and attractive way

unity

The state of being joined together or in agreement

empowerment

The process of gaining freedom and power to do what you want or to control what happens to you

The right of different groups of people to have a similar social position and receive the same treatment

our team

We, at Ducia, strive to create a fun and informative platform for young women, to help them develop both professionally and personally. We want to do so, by creating an event and online platform. The event would be interactive with workshops that teach self-love, self-defense, but also choosing career paths and finding your true self. The online platform is a way of getting in touch with others, they can read stories about experiences and get in touch with Ducia as well. We want the visitors to empower and talk to each other and spread their experiences.  

Are you excited and or curious? Then let's get your tickets to you favourite event! Do you still have questions? Please, do not hesitate to ask us. We are open to answer all your questions!
